# How to Take Attendance

## 1. Open the Attendance Screen
From the sidebar, click **Take Attendance**.

---

## 2. Pick a Date
Click any day on the calendar on the left. The selected date is highlighted in dark gray.

- Use the **← / →** arrows to move between months.
- Click **today** to jump back to the current date.
- Days that already have attendance saved show a **green number** — that's how many students were present.

---

## 3. Find Students
Use the **Search students...** box to filter by name. Useful for large rosters.

---

## 4. Mark Attendance

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| Mark a student present | Click the **Present** button next to their name — it turns green |
| Unmark a student | Click **Present** again — it returns to gray |
| Mark everyone present at once | Click **Mark All → Present** in the top-right of the student list |

The **Present / Unmarked** counts at the top update in real time.

---

## 5. Save
Click **Save Attendance** at the bottom right when you're done.

- You can save with **zero students marked** — this clears any previously saved records for that day (useful if you mistakenly saved on a day the school was closed).
- A green "Attendance saved." confirmation appears briefly after a successful save.

---

## Tips
- Clicking a date loads any **previously saved attendance** for that day automatically.
- You can come back to a past date, make changes, and re-save — it overwrites the existing record.
